## Who it is for
High school students, current college students, working adults finishing a degree,
career changers, and parents funding college.

## How credit stacking works (5 steps)
1. Map your target degree's 120 credits to requirements (general ed, electives, major).
2. Clear general-ed and elective credits with CLEP ($97/exam, free prep via Modern States), DSST, and self-study AP.
3. Fill gaps with ACE-approved providers (Sophia, Study.com) and PSEO/dual enrollment.
4. Verify every credit transfers BEFORE testing (the #1 rule).
5. Transfer the stacked credits into a degree-completion university (WGU, UMPI) to be conferred the bachelor's.
