Privacy Policy

Effective April 7, 2026

Overview

Executive Strategy Consulting LLC d.b.a. Fast Grad Academy ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates fastgradacademy.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you purchase the Fast Grad Academy digital guide, we collect:

  • Email address — to send purchase confirmation and provide your access code
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe; we do not store card details
  • Access code usage — to track when you access your purchased guide
  • Device information — browser type, IP address, and session data (for support purposes)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for:

  • Delivering your purchase — generating and sending your unique access code
  • Sending transactional emails — purchase confirmation, access links, account updates
  • Providing customer support — resolving access issues or technical problems
  • Preventing fraud and abuse — protecting the integrity of our digital product
  • Legal compliance — fulfilling any legal obligations or court orders

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Stripe — processes all payments securely. See their privacy policy.
  • Supabase — stores and manages access codes for our digital guide
  • Resend — handles transactional emails (purchase confirmations, access codes)

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and only receive the minimum information necessary to deliver their services.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal tracking:

  • localStorage — stores session data (your access code, preferences) locally on your device for convenience. This data never leaves your device.
  • No third-party cookies — we do not use tracking cookies or analytics pixels that follow you across the web

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary to:

  • Provide you access to your purchased guide
  • Respond to support requests
  • Fulfill legal and tax obligations (typically 7 years for payment records)

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to update or correct your information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at john@executivestrategy.consulting.

Data Security

We take security seriously. All payment data is encrypted and processed through Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure. Access codes are stored securely and are not shared with unauthorized parties.

No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with marketers, advertisers, or third parties for any reason. Your data is used exclusively to deliver our service and support your purchase.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that we've collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
