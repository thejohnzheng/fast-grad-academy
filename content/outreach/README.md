# Launch Day Outreach Content

This folder contains copy John can use for manual launch-day outreach on Monday, June 29.

The Day 1 target is:

- 25 direct touches
- 5 value-first posts

Use this content as a starting point. Personalize where possible. Do not add tracking URLs unless John or the PM adds them manually.

## Files

### Reddit Posts

Folder: `reddit/`

- `reddit-college-clep-guide.md` — value-first r/college post explaining CLEP and how to avoid wasted credits.
- `reddit-pf-college-savings.md` — r/personalfinance angle focused on debt avoidance, opportunity cost, and ROI.
- `reddit-debt-prevention.md` — reflective r/college or r/studentloans post about preventing debt before borrowing more.

Each Reddit file includes:

- 3 title options
- 300-500 word post body
- Comment reply template for when someone asks for the link

Important Reddit rule: do not put `fastgradacademy.com` in the main post body. Share it only in replies when people ask for more information.

### YouTube Community Post

File: `youtube-community-post.md`

### DM Templates

File: `dm-templates.md`

Includes 5 short templates:

1. Friends/family with college-age kids
2. LinkedIn connections in education
3. Former classmates
4. YouTube/newsletter subscribers who engaged
5. Relevant communities/groups

These are intentionally short. They should feel like a personal nudge, not a mass campaign.

## Voice Rules

- Write as John Zheng.
- Calm, specific, and personal.
- Use only verified numbers: `$39,633`, `$84,000+`, `$97`, `34 CLEP exams`, `2,900+ colleges`.
- Avoid marketing language.
- Do not use post-title emoji.
- Do not mention unrelated projects, holdings, or brands.

## Day 1 Suggested Flow

1. Publish 1 Reddit post in the morning.
2. Send 10 personalized DMs.
3. Publish the YouTube community post.
4. Send 10 more personalized DMs.
5. Publish 1-2 additional value-first posts if the first post is received well.
6. Send 5 final personalized DMs to warm contacts most likely to reply.

Keep replies helpful. If someone asks for the full system, share `fastgradacademy.com` naturally.
